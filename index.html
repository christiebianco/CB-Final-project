TEFAF | NEW YORK 2023

MAY 12 - 16, 2023

As the world’s most prestigious and vibrant art market, New York City provides the ideal setting for a TEFAF fair outside of Maastricht. We are proud to present the eighth edition of TEFAF New York at the Park Avenue Armory May 12–16, 2023. The historic Park Avenue Armory is a prime Manhattan location and setting for the world’s leading art dealers to showcase their work.
